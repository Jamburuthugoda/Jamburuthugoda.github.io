## Research

The Jamburuthugoda Lab studies enzyme structure-function relationships, with a particular focus on malate dehydrogenase (MDH) and related metabolic enzymes.

Our work integrates biochemical, kinetic, structural, and computational approaches to understand catalytic mechanisms and metabolic regulation.

### Current Areas of Interest

- Malate dehydrogenase from *Trypanosoma brucei*
- Malate dehydrogenase from *Trypanosoma cruzi*
- Structure-function relationships in mutant MDH enzymes
- Enzyme kinetics and mechanism
- Undergraduate research in biochemistry
